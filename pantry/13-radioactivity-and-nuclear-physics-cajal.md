### Fig 13.6 — Quantum Tunneling Through the Coulomb Barrier
- **Type:** Mechanism cross-section / energy diagram.
- **SCOPE:** X: distance from nuclear center (fm). Y: potential energy (MeV). Curve shape: deep nuclear well (strong-force attraction) at small r, rises through a Coulomb barrier (peak ~30 MeV at nuclear surface), falls off as 1/r outside. Horizontal line at α kinetic energy (~5 MeV). Wave function drawn — large amplitude inside well, exponentially decaying through barrier, small amplitude outside.
- **P:** 6-7 components: potential curve, energy line, three wave function regions (oscillating inside, decaying in barrier, oscillating outside), barrier annotation. Sky-blue for the wave function, vermillion for energy line.

- **Caption hook:** Author's caption captures it.
